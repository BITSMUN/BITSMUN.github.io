General Assembly

Agenda: 1. Discussing the criminal accountability of United Nations officials and peacekeepers while on a mission.
2. Contemporary law of the sea and the legality of targeted killings

With ever increasing acts of aggression, presence of non-state actors, flaring tensions and lack of trust, it is indeed the need of the hour for all countries to unite as one. BITSMUN Hyderabad’16 presents the General Assembly, the largest committee of the United Nations, the only one where all countries receive equal representation, where the largest economy or the largest military power is treated on par with the smallest of the developing economies.

Human Rights Council

Agenda: 1. Strengthening the international framework to combat human trafficking and migrant smuggling.
2. Discuss the rights and treatment of prisoners of war according to the Geneva Convention.

No freedom of expression, lack of equality, lack of proper nutrition and racism are all violations of the basic Human Rights that are inherent to every human being on Earth. This BITSMUN Hyderabad, unite to think of solutions to these issues in the Human Rights Council, where you fight for all that is human in nature.
"To deny people their human rights is to challenge their very humanity."
-Nelson Mandela

Futuristic Security Council

Agenda: Situation in the Middle-East in 2019 with special emphasis on tackling the growing violence in the Gaza Strip and Eastern Jerusalem on account of an increasing demand for a Palestinian nation.

The Security Council is the United Nations' most powerful body, thrusted with the responsibility of the maintaining international peace and security. At BITSMUN Hyderabad’16, we fast forward you into the future. Tensions have grown, trust in one another has declined and the problems are even more difficult to solve. In these tough circumstances, can you uphold the principles of the United Nations and potentially save the future? Do you have what it takes to be a true diplomat? If yes, then welcome to the Futuristic Security Council.

G20 Summit

Agenda: 1. Discussing the ripple effects of Eurozone crisis on refugee flows in Europe
2. Review of the effectiveness of Convention on Climate Change with special emphasis on COP21

"Money makes the world go round"
With the presence of 86% of the world's GDP combined, you have the oppurtunity to influence the world economy, rewrite the international law and redefine global politics. BITSMUN Hyderabad'16 welcomes you to G20, an international forum, witnessing the participation of the world's 20 biggest economies. Navigate your way through bilateral, multilateral agreements and overturn the Geo-political scenario discussing everything from terrorism to climate change. The fate of the world is in your hands. Are you up for the Challenge?

From the Secretary-General

Honourable future delegates, esteemed colleagues, dear friends,
As this year’s Secretary General, it is my distinct pleasure to welcome you to the fifth edition of BITSMUN, the Model United Nations Conference hosted by BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus. I shall have the privilege to be your humble host from 14th-16th October, 2016 in the wonderful city of Hyderabad. We, the BITSMUN Society, have always strived to host a conference that promotes quality in all substantive spheres.
BITSMUN transcends the boundaries of a conventional debate to become, for the delegates, a riveting platform to appreciate, acquire and practice the amazing art that is diplomacy. This edition of BITSMUN is filled with surprises and its contents will allow you, as a representative of a foreign government, to explore new issues ranging from human rights abuse to terrorism to border disputes, face new challenges and meet new people from diverse regions. If you’re a first timer, BITSMUN can provide you with the perfect blend of challenges and guidance. You can call this conference the perfect starting point for what can become your own MUN adventure. We are committed to our cause of fostering innovative ideas and promoting meaningful debate and arrive at path-breaking solutions to the numerous issues that have plagued our world. Some of these issues are so severe, that they possess the ability to wipe off life, from the only planet known to nurture itself.
BITSMUN 2016 in all aspects will certainly be an intellectual battle-field. New alliances will be formed, old alliances will break, foreign policies will be modified, laws will be amended, new ideologies will come into existence, the guilty will be prosecuted and justice will be served. So, if you think you can shed some light on global issues and you feel excited to debate intensively during the conference, experience the multiplicity of views on current international events, BITSMUN is the perfect conference to attend this year. Don your diplomatic attire, choose a country that you would like to represent and prepare for the ever-inspiring and rewarding talks. We invite you to leave your mark through your erudition, debating and public speaking skills.

The OC of BITSMUN’16 and I, once again cordially welcome you to these three days of intense debate, of stimulating challenges, of compromises and negotiations, of promises and allegations, of awkward ceasefires, of new experiences and lots of fun.


Neel Shah
Secretary General
